What We Do

Irrigation Services

We handle repairs, rebuilds, and anything in between — residential or commercial.

Irrigation Repairs

Broken heads, damaged supply lines, stuck or leaking valves — we diagnose the issue and fix it the same visit when possible.

Zone Rebuilds

Full zone rebuilds designed for proper head-to-head coverage, with the right equipment for your lawn size and soil type.

Leak Detection

High water bills and soft spots in the yard are signs of an underground leak. We locate and repair them before the damage spreads.

Backflow Repair

Licensed backflow preventer testing, repair, and replacement. We keep your system compliant with SAWS regulations.

Drip Systems

Drip irrigation installation and repair for gardens, flower beds, and landscaped areas — efficient watering without overspray.

Valve Locating

Buried valves that no one can find. We locate them so repairs and upgrades can get done without tearing up the yard.
